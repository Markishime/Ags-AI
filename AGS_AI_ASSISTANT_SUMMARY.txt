WHAT IS AGS AI ASSISTANT?
================================================================================

AGS AI Assistant is your personal farming expert that analyzes soil and leaf 
test reports using artificial intelligence. It reads your lab results, 
compares them to Malaysian Palm Oil Board (MPOB) standards, and provides 
detailed recommendations to improve your plantation's health and productivity.

Simple Process: Upload Lab Report → AI Analyzes → Get Recommendations

WHO IS IT FOR?
- Farm Managers making fertilizer decisions
- Estate Supervisors managing daily operations
- Agronomists providing professional advice
- Plantation Owners monitoring investments
- Fertilizer Suppliers understanding customer needs

4. REGENERATIVE AGRICULTURE INTEGRATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sustainable practices to reduce long-term fertilizer dependence:

✓ Empty Fruit Bunch (EFB) Mulching
  - Rate: 40-60 tonnes/ha/year
  - Adds organic matter and potassium naturally
  - Reduces MOP fertilizer needs by up to 100%
  - Improves soil structure permanently

✓ Leguminous Cover Crops
  - Plants capture nitrogen from air (FREE!)
  - Adds 100-150 kg nitrogen/ha naturally
  - Prevents soil erosion
  - Improves soil aeration

✓ Composting (EFB + POME)
  - Converts mill waste into rich fertilizer
  - Provides balanced, slow-release nutrients
  - Builds fertile topsoil

✓ Biochar Application
  - One-time application lasts decades
  - Increases nutrient holding capacity
  - Improves water retention
  - Reduces fertilizer runoff

Benefits:
- Lower fertilizer costs year after year
- Environmental sustainability
- Improved soil health over time
- Reduced dependency on chemicals
